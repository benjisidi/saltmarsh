# Description
## Appearance
Young Billy Connolly - long hair, scaggly appearance, wears the same three 40-year-old tweed suits that have been used for romping around the countryside.
## Persona
Eccentric old naturalist magic user.
Kindly and gentle, enthusiastic about the world. Well liked in [[Smaller Town]].
# Location
Lives in [[Smaller Town]] conducting plant and animal research and sometimes magic
# Additional Notes
Tutor to [[Tiresias]] for a few months before he arrived in [[Saltmarsh]]
