A huge river flows through every area of the spirit. From the waters of the river, there grew a gigantic oak tree. An acorn fell from that, and from there the first human came. All of the races are a branch off from that one human. Cultures from specific places tend to take on some aspect of the geography around them.

In the beginning, there was only the river Danue. Split from its water were the **first peoples**, demigod-like figures. The first peoples planted the great tree from which the humans were made. 

When the first peoples were made, a corresponding force emerged - the [[Daegon]].
