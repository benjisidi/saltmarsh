A huge river flows through every area of the spirit. From the waters of the river, there grew a gigantic oak tree. An acorn fell from that, and from there the first human came. All of the races are a branch off from that one human. Cultures from specific places tend to take on some aspect of the geography around them.

In the beginning, there was only the river [[Danu]]. Split from its water were the first peoples, *The Children of Danu*, demigod-like figures. The first peoples planted the great tree from which the humans were made.

When the first peoples were made, a corresponding force emerged - the [[Daegon]]: upwards rose The Children, and downwards sunk the Daegon, sealed away in the depths.

The *Children* are sort of nymph-level beings; they tend to be associated with a concept or thing in the same way gods of a pantheon would be, but are much less powerful.
